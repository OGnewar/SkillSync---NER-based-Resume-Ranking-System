Welcome to SkillSync – Resume Ranking with AI

At SkillSync, we bridge the gap between talent and opportunity using advanced Named Entity Recognition (NER) and AI-driven resume ranking. Our intelligent system analyzes resumes and job descriptions to deliver highly accurate candidate-job matching, ensuring recruiters find the best-fit applicants efficiently.


Our Mission

We aim to revolutionize the hiring process by leveraging AI and Natural Language Processing (NLP) to provide fair, transparent, and data-driven resume evaluations. With SkillSync, companies can reduce hiring time, improve candidate quality, and make informed decisions.


How SkillSync Works

  1. NER-Powered Resume Parsing

    2. Extracts key skills, experiences, qualifications, and certifications from resumes.

  2. NER-Powered Job Description Parsing

    3. Extracts key skills, experiences, qualifications, and certifications from job descriptions.

  3. Job-Resume Comparing Algorithm

    4. Compares resumes against job descriptions to generate a match percentage.

  4. Ranking Resumes

  • Time-Saving Automation

  • Fair & Unbiased
